Our Campuses

IILM University, Greater Noida

The Greater Noida campus is situated in the special educational zone known as Knowledge Park. The University, established under the Uttar Pradesh Private University (Amendment) Act 2022, envisions a Global Inclusive and Responsible approach, with a specific focus on Computer Science and Engineering, Technology, Innovation, and Entrepreneurship. The campus, located in 16-18 Knowledge Park II, Greater Noida, boasts a built area exceeding 26 acres, renowned for its state-of-the-art facilities. We offer a diverse range of programmes through various schools, including the School of Liberal Arts & Social Sciences, School of Management, School of Computer Science & Engineering, School of Engineering, and Law School. These schools contribute to creating a comprehensive and enriching academic environment

IILM University, Gurgaon

Our university, established under The Haryana Private Universities (Amendment) Act, 2018 (Haryana Act No. 10 of 2018), is situated in Sector 53 Gurugram, featuring a built area exceeding 1.5 lakh square feet and state-of-the-art facilities. The 7-acre Gurgaon campus encompasses computing facilities, lecture theatres, a library, residency facilities, and a student recreational centre. Offering diverse programmes, the School of Liberal Arts & Social Sciences, School of Management with AICTE-approved PGDM, School of Computer Science & Engineering, and the Law School, operate within this academic haven. These schools are supported by essential amenities such as a library, lecture theatre, computer centre, auditorium, and training centre, providing a comprehensive and enriching educational experience.

IILM Academy of Higher Learning, Jaipur

Established in 1998, IILM Academy of Higher Learning in Jaipur is committed to providing quality education to the youth. Our AICTE-approved Post Graduate Diploma in Management (PGDM) is offered at a well-located campus in the picturesque city of Jaipur. The institute aims to empower students with knowledge and skills, enabling them to make a significant impact in the dynamic business world. Our mission is to foster a cadre of talented managers who contribute meaningfully to global businesses. We offer a range of postgraduate programmes, providing aspiring managers with a comprehensive understanding of the business landscape. The curriculum is benchmarked against global standards, ensuring that our students receive an education that goes beyond traditional domains. In addition to core areas like Marketing, Finance, and Operations, IILM PGDM introduces specialisations in FinTech, Marketing Innovation, and Business Analytics. Our integrated and experiential PGDM curriculum incorporates the best practices from leading global B-schools, inspired by renowned institutions such as Harvard, Wharton, and Yale, including field courses, career treks, and community experiences. This approach ensures that our students receive a well-rounded education with practical insights. Since our inception, IILM Academy of Higher Learning has been instrumental in shaping the future entrepreneurs of tomorrow. With over a decade of experience, we take pride in our 3500+ well-placed alumni, who have made their mark in top companies both in the UK and abroad.

IILM Academy of Higher Learning, Lucknow

IILM Academy of Higher Learning, Lucknow, established in 2004, operates under the Ram Krishan & Sons Charitable Trust. It offers a two-year full-time Post-Graduate Diploma in Management (PGDM) & PGDM (Finance), both approved by AICTE. As part of a national brand, the institute, committed to quality professional education, has effectively disseminated its vision. The Lucknow Campus, integral to achieving this objective, significantly contributes to the institute’s national reputation, offering programmes such as PGDM and PGDM (Finance).
